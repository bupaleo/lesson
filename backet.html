Issyk-Kul

Issyk-Kul with its crystal clear water, mineral springs, magnificent sandy beaches and combination of sea and mountain climate is an excellent resort area with mud and thermo-mineral treatment. Healing water of springs and the lake itself is used in swimming pools, and the therapeutic sulfide silt mud in combination with natural and climatic factors of Issyk-Kul is used to treat many chronic diseases.

Kol-Top

The trek to Kol Tor is an easy dayhike to a beautiful mountain lake off the upper At Bashi Valley in the Naryn region of Kyrgyzstan. Short distances and minimal elevation gain make this suitable for trekkers of all levels, and the views stack up with some of the best hikes in all of Naryn.

Jailoo Sarala-Saz

Jailoo Sarala-Saz is an alpine pasture, located 54 km to the north-west of the village of Kochkor. It is situated at the altitude of over 3000 m. It's name, which is translated from Kyrgyz means "the yellowish swamp" the jailoo received because of the characteristic color of grass growing here and practically complete absence of shrubs and trees around.
